Instructions

The MetroGnome is a metronome which helps you practice odd rhythms.

In Tuplet mode, you can add clicks to subdivide each beat from 2-16 subdivisions

With PolyRhythm mode, you can have two seperate metronomes playing at once, with each dividing the bar into different divisions.

Usage

The MetroGnome can be run as a VST in your Digital Audio Workstation and syncs with your DAW's tempo. It can also be run as a standalone application.

Tuplet mode is like your standard metronome, but with the added option to subdivide each beat into tuplets

The tuplet can be anywhere from 2 to 8 subdivisions of the beat

You can also specify the amount of beats before the metronome plays a unique tick to signal a new bar.

Polyrhythm mode allows you play two rhythms at the same time, with each rhythm dividing the bar into different subdivsions

A unique click will play when both rhythms hit a beat at the exact same time.

PolyMeter mode is currently in development

With PolyMeter mode, you will be able to run two metronomes at the same time, each with their own time signature
