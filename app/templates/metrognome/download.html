Download MetroGnome

The MetroGnome is a metronome which helps you practice complicated rhythms. It can be downloaded as a standalone app or as a plugin for your DAW.

With Tuplet mode, you have a regular metronome which the addition of odd subdivisions of beats, from 2-16 subdivisions. You can automate this in your DAW to practice alternating subdivisions.

With PolyRhythm mode, you can practice with two contrasting rhythms dividing the bar into different numbers of beats.
